# Privacy Policy for Football Fan Tracker

**Last Updated: January 20, 2026**

## Summary

Football Fan Tracker does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal user data. Your team selection is stored only on your device.

## Data Collection

Football Fan Tracker does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal user data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and respects your privacy.

## Local Storage

The extension stores only your selected football team preference locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data:

- Remains on your device only
- Is never transmitted to any server owned or operated by us
- Can be cleared at any time by changing your team selection or removing the extension

## Third-Party Services

The extension fetches publicly available match data (scores, fixtures, team names, and logos) from TheSportsDB API (https://www.thesportsdb.com). When fetching this data:

- No personal information is sent to TheSportsDB
- Only team IDs are used to request match information
- Please refer to TheSportsDB's privacy policy for information about their data handling practices

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **Storage**: Used only to remember your selected football team locally in your browser
- **TheSportsDB Access**: Used only to fetch public match information (scores, fixtures, team data)

## Data Retention

Your team selection is stored locally in your browser until you:

- Change your team selection within the extension
- Clear your browser's extension data
- Uninstall the extension

## Data Sharing

We do not:

- Sell or transfer any user data to third parties
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Collect any personally identifiable information
- Track your browsing history or website activity

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the extension's functionality or legal requirements. Any changes will be reflected on this page and in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.

## Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Football Fan Tracker is an independent project and is not affiliated with TheSportsDB or any football league or organization.
